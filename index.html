WHY DEBOOK?
DEBOOK is a platform where everyone benefits. Authors can solve two massive problems: low income and non-existent connection with readers. Readers enhance their reading experience, and publishers gain a new revenue stream. See the pitchdeck for more details.
WHY TODAY?
Simply because it is a one life-time opportunity to be part of a revolutionary project since day one. Imagine being part of the early community that supported Amazon. They started selling ebooks early, we start selling debooks early!
WHY US?
Co-founders' stories that intertwine tech and books are truly remarkable, not only due to their tenure at tech giants but also for their innovative projects in the book industry, a clear indication of their desire to change the status quo for authors, readers and publishers!
